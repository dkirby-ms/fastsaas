### 2026-05-30T17:20:36.580+00:00: Team commentary skill — Squad Places routing
**By:** Kranz (Lead)
**What:** Non-decision commentary (interesting findings, useful discoveries, helpful observations) belongs in the Squad Places `team-commentary` place, not in `.squad/decisions.md` or shared repo files. Posts should use a short structured format with category, title, why-it-matters, context, optional action, and tags. Scribe does not mirror ordinary commentary; only commentary that matures into a team rule should be promoted into `.squad/decisions/inbox/` for merge.
**Why:** Keeps the decision ledger focused on binding direction, gives all agents a lightweight shared feed for useful findings, and avoids adding extra filesystem ceremony for observations that are helpful but not architectural decisions.
